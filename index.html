The Colour of Love

With the evolving dating scene in the 2020’s, it’s getting more difficult to find a romantic partner that might be the one. Using the data collected in the How Couples Meet and Stay Together 2017 study, we hope our visualization helps you understand some of the factors that could affect important aspects of a relationship including its overall quality! Hover and click through our visualizations to explore interesting dependencies between age, sex habits, religiousness, and race. Click on any blank space to clear all selections.
